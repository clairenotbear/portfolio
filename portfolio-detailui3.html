b-mobile

Travel SIM card APP Design

b-Mobile is the second company of Japan Communications Corporation, providing travel SIM cards for travelers or tourists. Users can use this application to purchase telecom solutions and SIM cards from different telecommunications companies.

The APP design uses the original brand colour of b-mobile, and the main design direction is convenient for users to purchase products. More directly, it is a consumer-oriented shopping-oriented app.
We set the travel location we want to promote in the "Featured Destination". This is the section that users will see first when they enter the app. It's because most of the users use "location" as their primary purchase filter.

We put telecom products in "Featured Data Plans", and some products of seasonal sales will be placed in this section to explore potential users who do not have any travel plans, click on these products with the psychology of seeking cheap plans.
