FANJIE is based on a local purple okra farm team in Taiwan.

They use organic agricultural technology to extract the essence of purple okra and extend it to the field of healthy tea and skincare products.

From natural soil to the table and daily care products, FANJIE pays great attention to purely natural ingredients. Therefore, in the design of LOGO, it combines human care and natural imagery.

The brand colour is set to the same violet as the purple okra. The Logo Type "FANJIE" is designed to be clean and degenerate to represent the company's reliable image, with grass green as a natural and gentle symbol.

-

"Corny Chicken Theatre" is the first professional troupe from the National Taiwan University Theatre. It was created by the director of the theatre group, Medusa Tu, in 2003, dedicated to the production of imaginative comedy.

The Chinese meaning of the troupe name "Corn Chicken" comes from the homonym of English "imagination". In addition to the extension of "imagination", it also represents the multi-lingual and multicultural drama development attempt of the troupe in the long-term planning. The phrase "Corny Chicken", which is translated by the chicken, turns to the vivid image of "a funny chicken" and also represents the troupe's creation of comedy. The troupe hopes to break the audience's stereotypes of children's dramas and present the professional theater aesthetics to the audience through stories full of childlike and profound meanings.
