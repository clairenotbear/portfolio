Feather Interior VI

VI Design

Feather interior design expects they're an interior design team with creative and rigorous and solid design foundation.

The founder told me the inspiration of this company name comes from the quill pen and ink, he thought although some traditional ways are more slowly and inconvenient, but the beautiful result of accumulation is irreplaceable in quick ways. No matter how fast the world is changing, some wisdom will never be changed.

Therefore, I use the quill pen as one of the design elements and create an impression of space with a colour block. This space is a partially open door, behind the door is an unknown world, waiting for the Feather interior design team to design for the customer with a traditional but flexible quill pen. The colour scheme is chosen to be presented in a stable and noble style.
