Online Customer Service

Online Customer Service widget for Desktop

When the user is using the product, unexpected events sometimes occur, enabling the customer to immediately contact the customer service team. The development of the customer service tool becomes important.

After fully communicating with the engineering team, the design task was to make this contact tool direct and simple to operate. There is not much fancy typography or operation, all the design purposes are designed to make the user easy to operate and understand.

Two operating conditions:
A. The user successfully connects with the customer service staff and start the online message conversation.
B. The user cannot successfully connect to the customer service staff for various reasons (non-business hours or other unknown reasons), the user will leave his contact information and message, the information will be transmitted to the customer service center via the company server.
